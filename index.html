The Beagle Blog

The Beagle is breed of small scent hound, similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. The beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare, known as beagling. Possessing a great sense of small and superior trackling instincts, the beagle is the primary been used as a detection dog for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world. The beagle is intelligent ans is a popular pet due to its size, good temper, and a lack of inherited health problems.

Trivia

  1. Height - 13-16 in (33-41 cm)
  2. Weight - 22–25 lb (10.0–11.3 kg)
  3. Coat -Short haired, hard coat of medium length
  4. Colour -Tricolor or white in combination with black & tan/brown or brown/tan
  5. Life span - 12–15 years
